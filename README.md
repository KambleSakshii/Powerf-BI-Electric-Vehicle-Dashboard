## üí° Project Overview
This project is a dynamic, interactive dashboard built to analyze the rapidly expanding Electric Vehicle (EV) market. The goal was to transform a large, complex dataset of EV registrations into **clear, actionable insights** for strategic business planning and market trend identification.

The dashboard focuses on answering critical business questions about market adoption, manufacturer dominance, and the shift from PHEV to BEV technology.

## üìä Key Findings & Insights
The analysis of **150,000+** vehicle registration records revealed several key insights:

* **Dominance Shift:** Confirmed the significant market shift, quantifying **78% Battery Electric Vehicle (BEV)** market share over Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV) across the dataset.
* **Critical Growth Period:** Identified the years **2020-2021** as a major inflection point for accelerating EV adoption.
* **Manufacturer Performance:** Tracked and visualized the performance of top manufacturers and their respective model popularity.
* **Range Analysis:** Provided a clear view of the **Average Electric Range** trend over time and across different vehicle makes.
